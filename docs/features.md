## Already Have

- Face tracking
- Landmarks
- Region masking (eyes, skin, lips, teeth)

## High-Impact Features to Add

### 1) Eye Enhancement (pairs perfectly with gaze fix)
- Eye whitening (reduce redness)
- Iris contrast boost
- Subtle sharpness
- Dark circle removal

**Why it matters:** Makes gaze correction look much more realistic.

### 2) Skin Retouch (must-have)
- Acne and blemish removal
- Skin smoothing (not plastic-looking)
- Texture-preserving blur
- Oil shine reduction

**Why it matters:** One of the most monetizable features.

### 3) Teeth Whitening + Smile Boost
- Teeth whitening (dynamic when mouth opens)
- Slight smile uplift (very subtle)
- Lip color correction

**Why it matters:** High value for YouTubers and talking-head content.

### 4) Auto Framing + Smart Zoom (MrBeast-style)
- Track face and keep centered
- Add dynamic zoom cuts
- Reframe for vertical formats (TikTok)

**Why it matters:** Pairs well with video-editor workflows.

### 5) Lighting and Color Correction (face-aware)
- Face relighting (simulate soft light)
- Skin tone correction
- Exposure balancing
- Remove harsh shadows

**Why it matters:** Makes poor footage usable.

### 6) Expression Correction (next-level)
- Reduce awkward expressions
- Slight confidence boost (posture + face)
- Smooth micro-expressions

**Why it matters:** Very powerful, but likely needs neural models.

### 7) Head Pose Stabilization
- Reduce small head shakes
- Smooth jitter
- Keep natural motion

**Why it matters:** Makes videos feel more professional.

### 8) Background Enhancement (low effort, high value)
- Background blur (depth of field)
- Replace background
- Clean messy rooms

### 9) Audio + Face Sync Enhancements
- Slight lip-sync correction
- Remove "uh / um"
- Match face cuts with audio cuts

### 10) Mask + Tracking System (core leverage)
